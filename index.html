Objective

To nurture the talent of underprivileged students of Class X (Sc.) at Orai by supporting them with scholarship for coaching from best coaching institutes for getting selected in premier engineering colleges and to support them financially during their stint in the engineering college.

About

HUL, Orai has played one of the most influential role in our professional careers and we all still do have an emotional bonding with Orai. We all have been successful in different capacities and have grown professionally and personally over the years. We firmly believe that the learning and experience at Orai have helped us in fulfilling our dreams to a great extnet. Hence, germination of this idea of giving something back to the place which has influenced us the most in our career and to fulfill the dreams of few of those children who are talented but are financially weak. Just imagine the “feeling of the immense satisfaction” if our efforts and contributions could bring a change in the lives of few children

working model

  • identification of students from government school(s) at orai of class x science stream basis their marks in the board exam
  • talent test to be conducted for students and as per the result the financial support(scholarship) to be given from the corpus for next 24 months for coaching through best coaching institutes in the region/kota etc.
  • the students will appear for competitive exams and if they get selected then we shall provide scholarship to them through education loan and corpus for next 4 years.
  • once the graduated students start earning and they will refund the money spent on them as per the agreement so that this project will be self-sustained on perpetual basis.
  • the process will continue every year for the fresh batch of students. volunteer(s)/part-time employee will manage the functioning of the corpus and will monitor the progress of the students.

Selection of Students

  • We will appoint one full time person on salary who will help in identifying the best government schools and will approach to the principal(s) to get the names of those students who are meritorious however need financial support to go for higher studies.
  • The salary of this person would be provided through the bank interest earned on the corpus
  • The selected students will appear for the talent exam and for the selection in coaching institute as per the working model stated earlier
