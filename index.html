Hello There

We Are WFR

Youth’s role is to simply to renew, refresh and maintain . The youth has the power to renew and refresh the current status of our society Keeping this in mind and hope in our hearts , carrying the torch of humanity ahead of us , we , writing for right come forward to work on the betterment for our society and for us . We, are a non profitable NGO completely based on social serving and strive to serve our people and bring a change within us. We believe that one act of kindness can change a lot in lives. Therefore keeping this as our agenda we come forward on having at least an impact to bring people towards good. Remembering to bring a change first we need to be the change, and having known that the youth is what we can call a change for our future ~ raising your voices can become a major turnaround of situations. If every soul tries to put in even the tiniest of efforts together we shall witness the world slowly turning better and healing from all the social evils. With that being said, come let's join hands together and together take a leap in working for the betterment of today's society, because, "Sometimes, reaching out and taking someone's hand is the beginning of a journey"-And this journey can ba turned into a revolution. Come join us and be a part of the change.
